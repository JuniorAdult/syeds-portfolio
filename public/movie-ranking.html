The Best Anime Recommendation According to Syed

Top 3 Anime to Start out with

Demon Slayer

As a beginner who is seeking for the best animations and a decent story to watch, I recommend watching demon slayer as its got breath taking animations and an amazing story.

Horimiya

If someone's out looking for a beautiful slice of life romance anime, I recommend watching Horimiya as its got the cutest couple of all time Hori-san and Miyamura. Its definetly a must watch.

Attack On Titan

Are you looking for a breath taking anime which is gonna keep you involved for almost 4 seasons and more? well worry not as youve got the most perfect anime in your hands to watch and waste a good few days of your life watching this.
